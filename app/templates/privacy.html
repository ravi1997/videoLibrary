Privacy Policy

Effective: {{ effective_date or '—' }} Version 1.0

This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information within the RPC Surgical Video Library (the “Service”).

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, role, identifiers, mobile (OTP).
  • Operational metadata: uploads, access logs, device/browser signals.
  • Security & audit data: failed logins, OTP attempts, moderation actions.
  • Content metadata: video tags, categories, annotations.

2. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate & authorize users.
  • Support playback, personalization, and recall (continue watching).
  • Security monitoring & abuse detection.
  • Operational analytics (aggregated, non-marketing).

3. Legal / Compliance

No PHI should be uploaded. Media must be de‑identified. Audit logs may be retained per institutional policies.

4. Data Sharing

No sale of personal data. Limited processors (SMS, indexing, storage) under contractual safeguards.

5. Security Measures

  • Role & verification gates.
  • Hashed credentials & short‑lived tokens.
  • Rate limiting & lockouts.
  • Segregated secret storage.

6. Retention

Data retained while active plus limited archival window unless policy or law dictates deletion.

7. Your Choices

  • Request corrections.
  • Request deactivation (subject to compliance retention).
  • Adjust personalization (settings page).

8. Cookies / Local Storage

Used only for session continuity, theme, and playback state—no third‑party ad tracking.

9. International Access

Cross-region access may occur; safeguards applied consistently though legal regimes vary.

10. Changes

Material policy updates will be communicated; ongoing use implies acceptance.

11. Contact

Reach administrators via designated secure channel for all privacy inquiries.

12. Summary

We minimize, secure, and purpose-limit data strictly to operate and improve the Service.
