## Welcome to RPC Surgical Video Library

Welcome to the RPC Surgical Video Library! This platform allows you to access and view surgical procedure videos for educational and professional purposes.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to use the system, even if you're not tech-savvy. We'll explain each step in simple terms with clear instructions.

## Getting Started

Before you can use the RPC Surgical Video Library, you need to create an account. There are two types of users:
- **Employees**: Staff members of the organization
- **General Users**: External users like students, researchers, or guest surgeons

Don't worry about which type you are - the system will guide you through the right process.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Access the Registration Page
1. Open your web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
2. Type the website address for the RPC Surgical Video Library : https://rpcapplication.aiims.edu
3. Look for a "Create a new account" button and click it

### Step 2: Choose Your User Type
You'll see two options:
- **Employee Registration**: If you're a staff member
- **General User Registration**: If you're an external user(account on base of mobile number)

Click on the option that applies to you.

### Step 2b: OTP Verfication
- OTP will be sent to the mobile according to user preferences. 
- Mobile number used for this will be unique and no two user can have same mobile number.

### Step 3: Fill in Your Information
You'll need to provide the following information:

**For All Users:**
- **Username**: Create a name you'll use to log in (3-50 characters)
- **Email Address**: Your valid email (we'll send important messages here)
- **Mobile Number**: Your phone number (we'll send security codes here)
- **Password**: A strong password (see password requirements below)

**For Employees Only:**
- **Employee ID**: Your unique employee identification number

**For Non Employees Only:**
- **Verification Document**: Upload a copy of ID card or salary slip or any other relavant document so that you can be verified by admin.

### Step 4: Create a Strong Password
Your password must be secure. It should have:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one capital letter (A-Z)
- At least one small letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Good Example**: "Surgery#2025"
**Bad Example**: "password" or "123456"

### Step 5: Complete Registration
1. Double-check all your information
2. Click the "Register" or "Create Account" button
3. You'll see a message confirming your registration was successful

### Important: Account Verification
After registration, your account needs to be approved by an administrator.

## Logging Into Your Account

Once your account is verified, you can log in to access the video library.

### Option 1: Traditional Login (Using Password)
1. Go to the RPC Surgical Video Library website
2. Find and click the "Login" button
3. Enter Your email address
4. Enter your password
5. Click "Login"

### Option 2: Mobile Login (Using OTP)
If you prefer not to remember passwords, you can log in using a one-time code sent to your phone:

1. Go to the RPC Surgical Video Library website
2. Find and click the "Login" button
3. Select "OTP Login" or "Mobile Login"
4. Enter your mobile number
5. Click "Send OTP"
6. Check your phone for a text message with a 6-digit code
7. Enter the code on the website
8. Click "Login"

## Managing Your Password

### Password Expiration
For security reasons, your password will expire every 90 days. When this happens, the system will ask you to create a new password during login.

### Forgotten Password?
Don't worry if you forget your password:

1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address or mobile number
3. Click "Reset Password"
4. Check your phone for a text message with a reset code
5. Enter the code on the website
6. Create a new password
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Submit"

### Password Best Practices
To keep your account secure:
- Don't use the same password as other websites
- Change your password regularly
- Never share your password with anyone
- If you think someone knows your password, change it immediately

## Understanding Your Account

### What You Can Do
As a regular user, you can:
- View published surgical videos
- Search for specific procedures
- Create playlists of favorite videos
- Track your viewing history

### Account Information
Your account profile shows:
- Your username
- Your contact information (email and mobile)
- Your account status (active or pending verification)
- When you last logged in
- What permissions you have

### Checking Your Account Status
To see your account information:
1. Log in to the system
2. Look for your name or profile icon in the top corner
3. Click on it to open a menu
4. Select "My Profile" or "Account Settings"

## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does account verification take?
A: Usually 1-2 business days. 

### Q: Can I change my username or email address?
A: No you cannot change them. Contact your system administrator to make these changes if very necessary.

### Q: What should I do if I don't receive the OTP text message?
A: Wait 5 minutes and try again. If it still doesn't work, check that you entered the correct mobile number.

### Q: Can I access the system from multiple devices?
A: Yes! You can log in from your computer, tablet, or smartphone.

### Q: What happens if I enter the wrong password too many times?
A: Your account will be temporarily locked for security. Contact your administrator to unlock it.

### Q: How do I log out securely?
A: Always click the "Logout" button when you're finished using the system, especially on shared or public computers.

### Q: Can I download videos?
A: For security reasons, downloading is not allowed. You can only stream videos within the system.

### Q: What should I do if I'm having technical issues?
A: Try refreshing the page first. If that doesn't work, contact your system administrator.

### Q: Is my personal information secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

### Q: Can I suggest videos to be added to the library?
A: If you have an Uploader Role then you can upload videos. If you want to have Uploader Role please contact your administrator.

### Q: What browsers are supported?
A: The system works best with the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

### Q: How much data will watching videos use?
A: Video streaming uses a lot of data. We recommend using Wi-Fi when possible.
